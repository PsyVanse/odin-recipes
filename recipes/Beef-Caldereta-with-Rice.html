Beef Caldereta with Rice

Steamed Rice with Beef Caldereta Soup

Ingredients
  • Beef Cut
  • Vinegar
  • Tomatoes
  • Potatoes
  • Ketchup
  • Rice
  • Garlic
  1. Cook the rice
  2. Boil the beef
  3. Mix the vinegar
  4. Steam the tomatoes and potatoes
  5. Mince the garlic
  6. Put in pot and add some ketchup
